About

    There is so much more to the field of Communicative Sciences and Disorders than just coursework and clinical skills! At the NYU Graduate NSSLHA Chapter, we focus on humanitarian efforts, advocacy, and public education for CSD. We do not let physical distance stop our chapter members from preparing for the future, as we are a collaborative group composed of both online and on-campus students. NYU Graduate NSSLHA is committed to promoting awareness of Communicative Sciences and Disorders through volunteering for the department, campus, and the community. NYU Graduate NSSLHA enhances the knowledge of its group members with speakers, experiences, and skills to help create well-rounded members of the CSD community.


Check out National NSSLHA's page for more info!
